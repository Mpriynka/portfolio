About Me

Hey there! I'm Priyanka More — a Computer Science undergrad at IIIT Pune who’s always been fascinated by puzzles, patterns, and the "why" behind how things work.

Growing up solving brain teasers, I’ve now leveled up to debugging complex problems and designing real-world solutions — whether through data structures & algorithms or building tools at hackathons.

I’ve had the thrill of winning the Smart India Hackathon 2024 and being selected as a Women Engineers Scholar by Talentsprint and Google. I love hackathons — they’re like playgrounds for ideas, and I enjoy turning abstract problems into working prototypes.

My curiosity often takes me under the hood of big systems — like how Git works or how interpreters evaluate code — and I try to recreate them from scratch. I tinker with languages like Scala, Elixir, and Haskell for the sheer joy of seeing things from a different lens. I’m also an AI/ML enthusiast, currently exploring how language models can be used to solve real-world problems.
